Using drone based photography at events, such as weddings, to allow clients to mark an occasion with dynamic, personalised photograph and video albums.

Who is it for?

Private and commercial event/wedding photographers.

What features does it have?

Come Fly With Me features a range of hardware and software solutions to provide truly striking drone photography and video.

Utilising state of the art stealth and camera technology to deliver a bespoke, candid and professional photography service, while ensuring the safety and comfort of guests.

Drone network offers live cloud backup to user as well as entire event site.

Come Fly With Me features
Category Feature Feature Description
Safety and Comfort Altitude Control Navigation sensors to move safely at all (reasonable) altitudes indoor and outdoor.
Collision Detection Advanced Collision Detection and Avoidance software to ensure safety of guests and property.
Quiet Brushless and soundproofed motors to reduce noise disruption.
Control Tracking Capability to run pre-set ‘follow-me’ routines targeted on an individual or individuals predefined through facial recognition.
Photography Lighting Light sensors to facilitate optimum photographic positioning.
Cameras High resolution 360-degree cameras offering multiple camera angles at same time, greatly adding to editing capabilities and enabling production of immersive, virtual reality wedding videos.
Angle Settings Pre-set routines include type of photography (panoramic/group, close-up), drone movement (e.g. constant hover for light optimisation or 360-degree circling) and roaming to capture all participants and the location.
Controls Coupled with separate mini-controllers to engage/disengage, return to base and change target or pre-set routine.
Netorking On board Wi-Fi network allows guests to add photographs taken by smartphone or camera.
