Concept Overview

MindFire's concept targets students, professionals and seniors interested in preserving and enhancing their memory functions, cognitive abilities and coping skills . It may be of particular benefit to people who have suffered a brain injury and need memory re-education. The users’ goals are to improve and develop their cognitive functions such as learning, reasoning, problem solving, understanding, remembering and paying attention, in an innovative and exciting manner. These improvements should result in improved mental health and quality of life. Our concept will achieve these objectives by offering:

  1. A variety of memory games and puzzles; these activities will strengthen memory and cognitive performance by forming adaptable thinking patterns and habits.
  2. Gamification and inbuilt social interaction features which will track and guide the user's progress through an interactive rewards system.
  3. A sleeping pattern tracker which will help overall memory processing.
  4. User access to recommended information on diet, exercise and mindfulness via an RSS feed-reader.

The core value of our concept is to improve the user’s overall well-being by enhancing and increasing their memory functions, cognitive abilities and lifestyle. Reduced brain function is a natural ageing process, even in people without medical conditions. Studies show that continued brain stimulation has many positive impacts on our general health and brain at any age. Also, regular exercise, good eating habits and mindfulness are a natural complement to memory training.

User Needs

MindFire is designed to strengthen memory and cognitive skills. These skills are universally beneficial, however they will be of most interest to the following groups:

  • Anyone who wants to improve their mental agility as part of their overall health and personal development, or simply enjoys puzzles.
  • People who feel the need to improve memory and problem solving to become more effective in work, or studying for exams or qualifications.
  • People who suffer from memory loss/deterioration due to illness, age or injury and who need to develop coping mechanisms to compensate.

With improvements in technology and medicine occurring at a considerable rate, people are living longer lives than ever before. Memory loss and dementia are increasingly affecting more people worldwide. As research builds a greater awareness and understanding of these conditions, MindFire will help people to adopt new habits and behavioural patterns, allowing them to make the most of their extended lives. By making these challenges easy, intuitive and enjoyable through gamification, we improve the chances of these memory enhancements becoming permanent in everyone’s life.

Functional Scope

MindFire allows users to improve their overall brain function through memory and cognitive-based games, puzzles and challenges. Tracking results and gamified reward system allow a clear view of progression and maintain the user's interest to encourage regular use. Progress is measured against personal targets, participating peers or an app-based model based on the user’s profile. Social Media integration creates a competitive edge that stimulates the users and can help in building a virtual community which is beneficial to psychological well-being.

The sleeping pattern tracker provides insight into how the user’s sleeping patterns can be improved to support better cognitive function.

The RSS feed-reader allows a user to access multiple information streams to complement their training.

Our concept doesn't compete with medical research or applications. MindFire is not a diagnostic or therapeutic application for debilitating mental diseases such as Alzheimer's, dementia or any other mental health impairment that affect the memory.

The roadmap for MindFire is to develop a lifestyle approach to improve memory and overall well-being. Cognitive games and regular sleep can improve memory, but the holistic approach brings it to the next level. To complement the RSS feed-reader, and provide more targeted lifestyle advice, we plan on creating API's allowing integration with recommended apps that offer relaxation techniques, mindfulness, physical activities and dietary advice. We are not looking to develop multiple apps. The aim of the project is to improve memory and cognitive function, supported by suggested lifestyle changes.
